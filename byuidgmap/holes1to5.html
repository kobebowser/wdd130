Holes One to Five

Hole 1

Hole one is a 288 ft par 3. The walking path on the right and beyond is out of bounds and so is the dirt path behind the basket and beyond.

Hole 2

Hole two is a 612 ft par 4. The dirt road on the left and beyond, and the fairway for hole six are out of bounds. If your disc lands on the fairway of hole six play from where it first crossed into that fairway

Hole 3

Hole three is a 465 ft par 4. The river rock across the fairway is out of bounds. The planters on the right and the fence and beyond on the left are also out of bounds.

Hole 4

Hole four is a 229 ft par 3. The walking path and beyond are out of bounds along with the brick wall on the right and beyond.

Hole 5

Hole five is a 226 ft par 3. The University Village parking lot on the right and beyond are out of bounds.
