Holes Six to Nine

Hole 6

Hole six is a 349 ft par 3. The wood chips on the left and beyond are out of bounds along with the rocks and beyond on the right. Disc has to stay on the grass in the middle. If the disc goes out of bounds, play from where the disc first crossed out.

Hole 7

Hole seven is a 420 ft par 4. The dirt road and beyond on the right are out of bounds.

Hole 8

Hole eight is a 514 ft par 4. The gravel path across the fairway, the sidewalk on the left and beyond, and the road past the basket are all out of bounds.

Hole 9

Hole nine is a 400 ft par 4. The gravel path across the fairway is out of bounds.
