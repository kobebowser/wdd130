BYU-Idaho Disc Golf Course

Satellite Map of Course

BYU-Idaho has recently introduced a course situated near the upper sports fields on their campus. The course is made of nine different holes. The majority of the course features open fairways, with approximately half of the holes being 400 feet or more from the tee to the basket. There's a significant amount of out-of-bounds areas as the course weaves around the fields and runs alongside a campus-owned apartment complex.
